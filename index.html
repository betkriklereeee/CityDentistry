Overview

A coastal brand refresh for San Clemente's most welcoming dentist.

This Statement of Work outlines the full-scope marketing engagement between NuvelloWeb LLC and City Dentistry. The campaign spans brand identity, digital marketing, community outreach, content creation, and performance measurement — all designed to grow new patient volume and deepen community presence along Del Mar Street.

24

Week Campaign

Phased rollout from brand launch through sustained community engagement and measurement.

6

Scope Areas

Brand refresh, SEO, social media, community events, content creation, and analytics.

3

Target Audiences

Young families, retirees, and young professionals in San Clemente and surrounding OC.

Campaign Timeline

24 weeks, phase by phase.

Each phase builds on the last — from brand foundation to sustained community presence.

Wk 1–4

Brand Refresh & Website Launch

New logo, style guide, tagline, and updated website go live. All communication assets released. Patient-facing materials updated.

Wk 2–8

Local SEO & Social Ad Campaigns

Google Business optimized, SEO strategy implemented, paid social campaigns launched on Instagram and Facebook targeting San Clemente residents.

Wk 4–12

Community Events & Outreach

Health fairs, school programs, and wellness initiatives activated. Local business partnerships established. Press releases distributed to OC media.

Wk 6–12

Measurement & Optimization

First performance reports delivered. New patient inquiry volume tracked. Campaigns adjusted based on early data and community feedback.

Wk 13–24

Sustained Growth & Reporting

Ongoing social media management, monthly reporting, continued SEO monitoring, and end-of-campaign evaluation with strategic recommendations.

Terms & Conditions

How we work together.

Payment Schedule

  • 50% deposit due upon signed agreement
  • Remaining balance invoiced upon project milestones
  • Monthly retainers billed on the 1st of each month
  • Net 15 payment terms on all invoices

Revisions & Approvals

  • Each deliverable includes rounds as specified above
  • Additional revisions billed at hourly rate
  • Client approvals required before proceeding to next phase
  • Feedback turnaround expected within 5 business days

Scope Changes

Any work outside this SOW will be scoped and quoted separately via a written change order before work begins. NuvelloWeb LLC reserves the right to pause work if invoices are outstanding beyond 30 days.

Ownership & Confidentiality

All final deliverables become the property of City Dentistry upon receipt of full payment. NuvelloWeb LLC may use completed work for portfolio purposes unless otherwise requested in writing.

Cancellation

Either party may terminate this agreement with 14 days written notice. All work completed to date will be invoiced. The deposit is non-refundable once work has commenced.

Timeline & Delays

Project timelines are contingent on timely client feedback and asset delivery. Delays caused by the client may result in adjusted delivery dates. NuvelloWeb LLC will communicate any delays promptly.
