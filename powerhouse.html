How This Works

The tools below are the infrastructure layer of your campaign — platforms and services that City Dentistry subscribes to directly. NuvelloWeb configures, manages, and optimizes each one as part of the monthly retainer, but every invoice from these vendors goes straight to you. No markup, no middleman fees.

Total estimated monthly third-party cost: ~$500/mo, of which $300–$600 is optional ad spend that scales with your growth goals. The non-ad infrastructure runs at roughly $85–$140/mo — less than a single new patient visit.

🌐
Web Hosting
Infrastructure · SiteGround or WP Engine — client pays direct
$25–$45
per month

Web hosting is the server where your website lives. Without it, your site doesn't exist on the internet. SiteGround and WP Engine are two of the most trusted managed WordPress hosts — both optimized specifically for WordPress performance, security, and uptime.

A slow or unreliable host tanks your Google rankings and drives visitors away before they even read a word. Managed WordPress hosting handles automatic updates, daily backups, and security patches so your site stays fast, secure, and online — always.

  • 99.9%+ uptime guarantee — your site is always reachable
  • Automatic daily backups — restore in one click if anything goes wrong
  • Free SSL certificate — required for Google trust signals and patient data security
  • Staging environment — test changes safely before pushing live
  • WordPress-optimized servers — significantly faster load times vs generic hosting
🔗
Domain Name
Infrastructure · GoDaddy, Namecheap, or Google Domains — client pays direct
~$15–$20
per year

Your domain is your web address — citydentistry.com or similar. It's the permanent home of your brand online, separate from your hosting provider.

A professional domain signals legitimacy to both patients and Google. A branded email address ([email protected]) built on your domain is far more trustworthy than a Gmail address for patient communications and insurance correspondence.

  • Permanent brand asset — you own it as long as you renew
  • Enables professional branded email via Google Workspace
  • Directly tied to your SEO authority — age and consistency matter
  • Transfer-friendly — not locked to any one host or provider

Google Workspace
Communication · Google — billed directly to client
$12–$18
per month

Google Workspace gives City Dentistry a professional email address (e.g. [email protected]), along with Google Drive, Docs, Calendar, and Meet — all under one roof.

A branded email address is non-negotiable for a professional practice. It builds immediate trust with patients, looks credible on insurance and referral paperwork, and keeps all practice communications organized and secure. Google Workspace also integrates natively with Google Business Profile and Analytics.

  • Professional branded email — no more @gmail.com on patient communications
  • 30GB+ cloud storage per user via Google Drive
  • Shared calendars for appointment coordination
  • Native integration with Google Business Profile and Analytics
  • HIPAA-compliant email options available with Business Starter plan

Email Platform (Mailchimp)
Marketing · Mailchimp — billed directly to client
$20–$50
per month

Mailchimp is the platform that sends City Dentistry's monthly patient newsletters and appointment reminder campaigns. It manages your patient email list, automates sequences, and tracks open rates and clicks — all from a simple dashboard.

Email remains the highest-ROI marketing channel in healthcare. A monthly newsletter keeps existing patients engaged, reminds lapsed patients to rebook, and builds the kind of ongoing relationship that drives referrals. Mailchimp's free tier covers up to 500 contacts — most practices can start there.

  • Drag-and-drop email builder — uses the branded HTML template we create
  • Patient list segmentation — send different messages to new vs returning patients
  • Automated appointment reminders and follow-up sequences
  • Open rate, click rate, and unsubscribe tracking
  • Free up to 500 contacts — scales affordably as your list grows

Meta Ad Spend (Instagram & Facebook)
Paid Media · Meta — billed directly to client's card on file
$300–$600
per month (recommended min. $300)

This is the actual budget that runs your Instagram and Facebook ad campaigns. Separate from NuvelloWeb's management fee, this money goes directly to Meta and determines how many people see your ads, how often, and in which locations.

Ad spend is the fuel — management is the engine. At $300/mo targeting San Clemente and surrounding OC zip codes, you can realistically reach 10,000–30,000 local impressions per month. At $600/mo, that doubles. The more you spend (to a point), the more new patients you reach. NuvelloWeb optimizes every dollar.

  • Geo-targeted to San Clemente and surrounding OC zip codes
  • Demographic filters — age, family status, homeownership, health interests
  • Retargeting — re-serve ads to people who visited your website
  • Billed to your Meta Business account — full transparency, no markup
  • Pause or adjust budget at any time — no long-term commitment to Meta

Google Analytics 4 & Search Console
Analytics · Google — no cost
Free
always

Google Analytics 4 (GA4) tracks who visits your website, where they came from, what they looked at, and whether they called or filled out a form. Google Search Console shows exactly which search terms are bringing people to your site and how you rank for each one.

You can't improve what you don't measure. These two free tools are the backbone of every monthly performance report — they tell us which campaigns are working, which pages are converting visitors into inquiries, and where to focus next month's effort.

  • Real-time and historical website traffic data
  • Conversion tracking — calls, form fills, appointment clicks
  • Search Console keyword rankings — see exactly what you rank for
  • Page performance reports — load speed, bounce rate, session duration
  • Completely free — no subscription, no usage limits
