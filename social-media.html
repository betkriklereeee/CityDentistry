The Strategy

Social media for a dental practice isn't about going viral. It's about showing up consistently in front of the right people — San Clemente families, young professionals, and retirees — in a way that builds familiarity and trust long before they ever need a dentist.

We run two parallel tracks: organic content (your ongoing presence, voice, and community connection) and paid ads (targeted campaigns that put City Dentistry in front of new audiences who don't know you yet). Each reinforces the other.

Organic Social Media — The Process
01

Account setup & brand alignment

Instagram, Facebook, and Google Business are fully optimized — bios, profile photos, highlights, and linked booking — all reflecting the new City Dentistry brand identity.

02

Monthly content calendar

We plan and create up to 12 posts per month across platforms. Content pillars include: patient comfort & experience, team highlights, oral health tips, community involvement, coastal lifestyle, and before/after (with consent).

03

Creative production

Each post is designed using City Dentistry's brand system — consistent colors, typography, and tone. Captions are written with local SEO hashtags and CTAs that drive profile visits, DMs, and appointment bookings.

04

Scheduling & community management

Posts are scheduled in advance for optimal times. Comments and DMs are monitored and replied to to build genuine community engagement — the kind that algorithms reward with more reach.

05

Monthly reporting

Every month you receive a clear report: reach, engagement, follower growth, top-performing posts, and recommendations for the next month based on what worked.

How Paid Ads Work

Instagram and Facebook ads allow us to target very specific audiences — people within a defined radius of City Dentistry's Del Mar Street location, filtered by age, family status, interests, and behaviors. Unlike print ads or billboards, you only pay to reach people who actually fit your patient profile.

We create the ad creative, write the copy, set up the targeting, launch the campaigns, and optimize them based on performance data — all included in the monthly retainer. The ad spend itself goes directly to Meta (Instagram/Facebook) and is a separate, client-paid budget.

The Ad Process
01

Audience research & targeting setup

We build custom audiences based on geography (San Clemente + surrounding OC zip codes), demographics (families, ages 25–65), and interests (health & wellness, family, home ownership). Lookalike audiences are created from your existing patient email list if available.

02

Ad creative & copy

We produce up to 2 ad creatives per ad set — static images and short-form video or carousel formats. Copy is tested with different hooks (comfort-focused, coastal lifestyle, new patient offer) to see what resonates.

03

Campaign launch & A/B testing

Campaigns run across up to 3 ad sets simultaneously, each targeting a slightly different audience or using different creative. We let data determine the winner and shift budget accordingly.

04

Optimization & scaling

Weekly performance checks — cost per click, cost per lead, click-through rate. Underperforming ads are paused. Winners are scaled. Ad copy and creative are refreshed monthly to avoid audience fatigue.
