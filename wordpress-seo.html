WordPress & Envato — What It Is

Your website will be built on WordPress — the world's most widely used website platform, powering over 40% of all websites. It gives you a professional, fully editable site without locking you into a proprietary builder like Wix or Squarespace.

Rather than building from scratch (which takes months and costs significantly more), we use a premium Envato template — a professionally designed theme purchased from Envato Market. These themes are built by world-class designers and come with pre-built layouts, animations, and components. We then customize it fully to match City Dentistry's new brand identity, colors, and content.

The result: a custom-looking, high-quality website delivered in weeks, not months — at a fraction of the cost of a bespoke build.

The Build Process — Step by Step
01

Template selection & purchase

We browse Envato Market together and select a template that fits City Dentistry's coastal, clean aesthetic. The ~$69 template fee is paid directly by you and is the only third-party cost for the build itself.

02

Hosting & domain setup

We configure your hosting environment (SiteGround or WP Engine), connect your domain, and install WordPress and the theme. Your site has a staging environment — meaning we build and test privately before anything goes live.

03

Brand customization

We apply City Dentistry's new logo, color palette, typography, and imagery throughout. Every page is adapted — not just the homepage. Up to 8 pages are built out: Home, Services, About, Patient Comfort, Community & ESG, Insurance, Contact, and Forms.

04

Copywriting & content

Every page gets written copy tailored to City Dentistry's voice — warm, coastal, and professional. Services are described clearly. The Patient Comfort guide and Community & ESG pages are written from scratch to reflect Dr. Dang's values and practice philosophy.

05

Digital forms & integrations

Patient intake forms are embedded directly on the site — no paper required. Google Business Profile is connected, updated, and optimized as part of the build.

06

Review, revisions & launch

You review the full site on staging. We incorporate your feedback, then flip the switch and go live. Post-launch, we monitor for any technical issues in the first 48 hours.

Learn More · On-Page SEO

Getting Found
on Google.

What Is On-Page SEO?

On-page SEO is everything we do inside your website to help Google understand what your practice does, who it serves, and where it's located. Done right, it means City Dentistry appears when someone in San Clemente searches "dentist near me," "teeth whitening San Clemente," or "family dentist Del Mar Street."

Most dental websites are invisible to Google — not because they're bad, but because no one took the time to set them up correctly. We fix that systematically, page by page.

What We Do — On-Page
01

Keyword research

We identify exactly what terms San Clemente residents search when looking for a dentist — by volume, competition, and local intent. We target keywords you can actually rank for, not just the most competitive ones.

02

Meta titles & descriptions

Every page gets a unique, keyword-optimized title and description — the first thing Google and searchers see. These are not generic. Each one is written specifically for that page's content and target search term.

03

Header structure & content optimization

We structure every page with proper H1, H2, and H3 headings that guide both readers and search engines. Copy is written to include natural keyword placement without feeling stuffed or unnatural.

04

Local schema markup

We add structured data (schema) that tells Google: this is a dental practice, here is the address, here are the hours, here is the phone number. This directly improves your Google Maps and local pack visibility.

05

Image optimization & alt text

Every image is compressed for fast load times and given descriptive alt text. Page speed is a direct Google ranking factor — slow sites rank lower, period.

Building Your
Authority Online.

What Is Off-Page SEO?

Off-page SEO is everything that happens outside your website to build its credibility in Google's eyes. The primary currency here is backlinks — other websites linking to yours. Google treats these like votes: the more reputable sites that link to City Dentistry, the more Google trusts your site and the higher it ranks.

For a local dental practice, off-page SEO is also about directory listings and citations — making sure City Dentistry appears consistently across every major local directory with the correct name, address, and phone number (NAP consistency).

What We Do — Off-Page
01

Google Business Profile optimization

Your GBP listing is the single most important local SEO asset. We fully optimize it — services, photos, posts, Q&A, categories, and hours — so City Dentistry shows up prominently in the local map pack.

02

Directory listings & citations

We submit City Dentistry to the top local and dental-specific directories: Yelp, Healthgrades, Zocdoc, Vitals, WebMD, Yellow Pages, Bing Places, Apple Maps, and more. Consistent NAP data across all listings signals legitimacy to Google.

03

Local backlink outreach

We identify local San Clemente and OC websites — community organizations, local blogs, business associations, school websites — and pursue relevant, high-quality backlinks that improve domain authority without risking Google penalties.

04

Review strategy

Google Reviews are a major local ranking signal. We implement a systematic review request process — email templates, in-office prompts, and follow-up sequences — to grow your Google and Yelp review count consistently.

05

Press & community mentions

Every local event, sponsorship, or press release we create also serves as an SEO asset. Coverage in local media and community sites generates organic backlinks that no ad spend can replicate.
